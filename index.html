About UsPermalink

We are a group of researchers based at Oakland University studying Natural Language Processing (NLP). We are committed to:

  • working on socially relevant research problems using state-of-art NLP methodology;
  • learning from one another to become better researchers, engineers, and educators;
  • effectively communicating our findings to the research community and general public;
  • engaging in ethical research practice;
  • providing a supportive work environment for all team members.
